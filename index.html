Doggie FAQs

How often should I walk my dog?

Most dogs benefit from 30 to 60 minutes of exercise per day, but it depends on their breed, age, and health. High-energy breeds may need much more!

What human foods are toxic to dogs?

Grapes, raisins, chocolate, onions, garlic, macadamia nuts, and foods containing the sweetener xylitol are highly toxic to dogs and should be strictly avoided.

How do I start training my new puppy?

Start with basic obedience commands like sit, stay, and come using positive reinforcement (treats and praise). Keep sessions short and fun!
